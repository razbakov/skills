# Delivery checklist (hand to user after build)

After the pack is built and QRs verified, deliver this to the user so they know exactly what to do with the files:

## Print

- **A4 4-up sheets** (`*-a4-4up-front.pdf` + `*-a4-4up-back.pdf`): print 5–10 copies double-sided on 200–300 gsm paper. Cut along crop marks with a rotary cutter for clean edges. Gives you 20–40 A6 flyers.
- **Vendor bleed master** (`*-flyer-front-bleed.pdf` + `*-flyer-back-bleed.pdf`): send to a print shop (Flyeralarm, Printpeter, local print shop) for higher volume. 300 gsm matte or silk, 3 mm bleed is already baked in.
- **Tent card** (`*-tent-card-bleed.pdf`): print one on 250+ gsm card, score along the horizontal midline (lightly cut halfway through), fold into an upside-down V, stand on the DJ booth / bar.

## Digital

- **IG story** (`*-instagram-story.jpg`): post to Instagram Stories 24–48 h before event, again the morning of. Add a location sticker and a link sticker with the event URL.

## Placement strategy (pre-event)

- Tent card on the DJ table the night of — both halves read correctly because the top half is mirrored.
- A6 flyers: 5 to the venue bar/host, 5 kept on you for hand-off when people ask "what is this?" Don't scatter on tables — looks desperate.
- IG story: evening before + morning of.

## Post-event follow-up

- Screenshot QR scan analytics (if the event URL supports it) to judge which channel drove signups.
- Keep one flyer as a physical artifact for your archive.
