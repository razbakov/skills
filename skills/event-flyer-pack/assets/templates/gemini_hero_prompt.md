# Hero image prompt template (image-from-gemini)

Use for the hero art that appears on the flyer front, tent card, and IG story. Generate **2 variants** and pick the one with more dark/low-contrast space in the bottom third — that's where event copy + QR will overlay.

## Prompt skeleton

```
{scene description — crowd, venue, mood, era}
Style: {editorial / cinematic / vintage poster / photo-illustration}
Composition: vertical portrait, 2:3 aspect. Leave the bottom third dark or
low-contrast with gentle vignette so large cream-colored text and a
20–24mm QR code can overlay cleanly.
Color palette: {warm terracotta and cream / wine red and navy / ...}
Lighting: warm practicals, soft rim light, no harsh flash.
NO text in the image. NO logos. NO watermarks. NO overlays.
No hands holding phones. No modern tech visible.
```

## Checklist before accepting

- [ ] Bottom third is dark enough that cream text will read (check histogram visually).
- [ ] No focal face in the dead center — QR goes there on the IG story.
- [ ] No text, no logos, no watermarks baked in.
- [ ] Portrait 2:3 or taller (gets cropped, not stretched).
- [ ] Mood matches the brief (if brief says "intimate bar scene", the crowd should be small and close, not arena-scale).

## Good reference prompts

**Cuban party, editorial:**
> Intimate Cuban bar scene in Munich, silhouettes of dancers in the foreground facing a vintage standing microphone, warm terracotta walls in soft focus behind them, hanging pendant lights, 1950s Havana aesthetic, cinematic editorial photography, vertical portrait 2:3, bottom third in deep shadow with soft vignette for text overlay, no text, no logos.

**Salsa social, photo-illustration:**
> Warm Tuscan-style living room converted to a social dance floor, couples mid-turn in casual clothes, golden hour light through French doors, hand-painted illustration style with soft brush texture, vertical portrait 2:3, bottom 30% fades to warm shadow for copy, no text, no logos.
